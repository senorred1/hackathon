About the Practice

We Are Not
Just a Dentist

CHOMPERZ™ was founded in 2019 when Dr. Crystal Molarstone had a vision — a vision involving a lot of teeth, some fog machines, and an overwhelming sense that dentistry was being done wrong.

We are a full-spectrum dental experience studio located inside a former laser tag arena that still kind of smells like laser tag. We've added plants. Most of them are real.

Our philosophy: Your mouth is a portal. We treat it with the reverence it deserves — using state-of-the-art equipment, artisanal numbing gel, and a Spotify playlist that Dr. Molarstone personally curates every single morning at precisely 6:44am.

We believe in radical tooth transparency. If we see something, we say something. Even if it's just that your bicuspid has excellent energy and is clearly the main character of your mouth.
